Pay HSBC Bank Credit Card bills online from any bank account

Pay your HSBC outstanding in 3 simple steps:

  1. Provide your Card Details

    • Enter your 16 digit HSBC Card number and the amount you would like to pay
    • Select your bank account from which you would like to execute this payment
    • You will be securely re-directed to the payment interface of your chosen bank


  2. Confirm Payment

    • Enter your authentication details
      (Net Banking User ID and Password of your Bank Account/Equivalent details)
    • Confirm payment amount
    • Your account will get debated instantaneously


  3. Receive online confirmation

    • You will receive an online transaction confirmation and a Transaction Reference Number
